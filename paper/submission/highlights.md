# Highlights (Elsevier: 3–5 bullets, ≤ 85 characters each incl. spaces)

- Sexual-violence exposure raises all 12 substance outcomes in 2023 YRBS (N=20,103)
- Prevalence ratios rise with substance severity: 2.2 (alcohol) to 7.9 (injection)
- Sadness mediates 15–23% of substance pathways and 46% of low connectedness
- Household ACEs show strict dose–response gradients on every outcome
- Calibrated 13-item screener finds 81% of at-risk students; fairness audited
